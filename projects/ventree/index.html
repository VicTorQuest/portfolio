Description

Ventree is a mobile-first bookkeeping and inventory management platform built for Nigerian small businesses. It helps SMEs easily track sales, expenses, debts, and stock in real time, generate simple profit and loss reports, and reduce revenue loss from forgotten credits through automated reminders. Ventree focuses on simplicity, accuracy, and local business realities to help owners make better financial decisions.
